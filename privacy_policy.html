Privacy Policy for Smarter Notes

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Developer: Dhruv Chaurasiya

Summary: Smarter Notes is a completely offline, privacy-focused note-taking app. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All your notes remain private and stored only on your device.

Introduction

Smarter Notes ("the App") is a simple note-taking application designed to help users create and manage their personal notes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect any personal information.

The App operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user behavior
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Require user accounts or registration

Data Storage

All notes and data created in the App are stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite database (stored in your device's internal storage)
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is backed up to cloud services
  • All data remains on your device only

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your notes on your device's local storage

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and no data leaves your device.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No internet connection is required or used
  • Your notes are private and accessible only to you
  • Data is protected by your device's security measures

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Uninstalling the App will delete all associated data
  • You can manually delete individual notes within the App
  • Clearing the App's data from your device settings will remove all notes

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This App complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Amazon Appstore Developer Agreement
